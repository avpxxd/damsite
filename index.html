The Emotional Backstory has been Written!

The story of how DamVan Came to be... is a very interesting one as you'll see.
The story starts in college, Where Will, Dalton, and Andrew got together at the newly opened E-Sports Lounge, where they played games and hatched ideas
for their group endeavors, which eventually turned into, well This! These three men have been through all the
nonesense of anything you might be able to think of, and also have been hosting a Minecraft Server, A fileshare server,
and of course, this website, while working on content for their group Youtube Channel, and then just being young kids
having fun with computers like they always did, but now combinding their briallance together to break the bounds
of technology. Their end goal is to develop a Tech Start-up, and continue to keep the ridiculousness from DamVan.
These men are all very taletned in their own unique ways, alone...they are easily underestimated, but together, they are unstoppable,
even pulling off a class prank changing all the wallpapers on their classmates computers. These men without a doubt are definitely able to
handle any task that you throw their way with their combined knowledge. Give them a chance and you will not be disappointed.
